Landmarks
Posted by Gloria Solinas on December 12, 2023

Scattered with lush green parks and gardens, Sofia nestles in the shadow of Mt. Vitosha, a hiking and winter sports destination. Roman ruins, onion-domed churches, neoclassical architecture, and Byzantine relics compete for attention with communist-era brutalist architecture.
