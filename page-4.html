Winter

Winter in the region, December to February, is cold with an average temperature just below freezing (0 °C or 32 °F). Stable conditions often cause fog, and while snowfalls are common, they are usually light, though occasionally heavier. Snow cover lasts for extended periods.

Spring

Spring, March to May, is erratic, particularly in March and April, with fluctuating temperatures and occasional snow. By May (and sometimes late April), warmer days emerge, reaching highs near 30 °C (86 °F), accompanied by the season's first afternoon thunderstorms.

Summer

Summer, June to August, is warm and sunny with cool nights and occasional afternoon thunderstorms. While daytime temperatures are generally warm, heat waves, reaching about 36/38 °C (97/100 °F), are short-lived. Humidity is low, and nights remain cool.

Autumn

Autumn, from September to November, starts pleasantly but turns colder, grayer, and relatively rainy by mid-October. Colder nights and the first snowfalls usually arrive in November, accompanied by increased fog.

SOFIA

Sofia is the capital and largest city of Bulgaria, a country in southeastern Europe. While it might not be described as a “magical” city in a literal sense, Sofia does have a rich history and diverse cultural heritage that can captivate visitors.

Things to know before you travel

Transport

Sofia has four fast and affordable ways of moving around; subway, buses, trolleybuses, and trams. If you are catching the last transport, make sure you get off at the final stop, otherwise, you might find yourself being driven to the depot rather than where you want to go.

Currency

The currency in Bulgaria is the Bulgarian lev. Each lev is divided into 100 stotinki. When you’re buying currency for Bulgaria, look out for the currency code BGN. And once you’re in Bulgaria, you’ll see the symbol лв used to show prices.

Weather

The average temperature is of 21.7 °C (71 °F), with a minimum of 14.3 °C (57.8 °F) and a maximum of 29.1 °C (84.4 °F). On the coldest nights of the month, the temperature usually drops to around 8.5 °C (47.5 °F).
