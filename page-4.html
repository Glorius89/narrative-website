Things to know before you travel

Transport

Sofia has four fast and affordable ways of moving around; subway, buses, trolleybuses, and trams. If you are catching the last transport, make sure you get off at the final stop, otherwise, you might find yourself being driven to the depot rather than where you want to go.

Currency

The currency in Bulgaria is the Bulgarian lev. Each lev is divided into 100 stotinki. When you’re buying currency for Bulgaria, look out for the currency code BGN. And once you’re in Bulgaria, you’ll see the symbol лв used to show prices.

Weather

The average temperature is of 21.7 °C (71 °F), with a minimum of 14.3 °C (57.8 °F) and a maximum of 29.1 °C (84.4 °F). On the coldest nights of the month, the temperature usually drops to around 8.5 °C (47.5 °F).
