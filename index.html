SOFIA

Sofia is the capital and largest city of Bulgaria, a country in southeastern Europe. While it might not be described as a “magical” city in a literal sense, Sofia does have a rich history and diverse cultural heritage that can captivate visitors.

Posted by Gloria Solinas on December 12, 2023

Interesting facts about Sofia

Did you know?

Sofia is nestled in the foot of a mountain

Sofia lies at the foot of Mount Vitosha which adds to its natural beauty. The mountain stands as a guard to the city in many ways including protecting it from harsh climatic conditions. Yet it offers a breathtaking backdrop and is a playground for outdoor adventure.

Sofia’s population is diverse

The dominant ethnic group in Sofia is Bulgarians. Yet the population also consists of significant minority groups such as Turks, Roma, and Russians. The largest minority group is the Bulgarian Turks who account for attest 10% of the country’s entire population.

Sofia is one of Europe’s greenest capitals

This city has earned a reputation for sustainability and commitment to greenery. This has made Sofia a remarkable green oasis with an extensive network of parks and lush green spaces making it one of the greenest capitals in Europe.
